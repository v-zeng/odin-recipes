Chicken Stir Fry Recipe

Description

This is a rainbow chicken stir fry recipe from Made With Lau. This dish has a fantastic balance of visual appeal, mouth-pleasing taste, and healthy ingredients.

There are many variations of this dish. The meat or vegetables can be substituted to your liking!

Ingredients

  • 10 oz chicken breast
  • 0.5 red bell pepper
  • 0.5 green bell pepper
  • 0.5 carrot
  • 0.5 onion
  • 2 celery stalk
  • 10 baby corn
  • 1 tsp ginger (slice thinly)
  • 1 tsp garlic (mince)

Instructions

  1. Prepare chicken and marinade (marinade recipe update coming)
  2. Prepare vegetables
  3. Heat stove and create cornstarch mix
  4. Partially cook chicken
  5. Cook vegetables
  6. Add flavors
  7. Add chicken and water before covering wok
  8. Add final cornstarch mix
  9. Serve immediately
