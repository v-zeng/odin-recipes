Egg Fried Rice Recipe

Description

This is an egg fried rice recipe from Made With Lau. Fried rice is a staple of Chinese cuisine. It typically seems to taste better when eating at a restaurant. That's probably because you haven't tried this recipe yet!

Here we use fresh rice instead of day-old or refridgerated rice. This recipe comes from a Chinese chef with over 50 years of experience, who has honed it over time to get that perfect taste.

Ingredients

  • 14 oz uncooked jasmine rice
  • 14 oz water
  • 1 oz green onion (just the green part)
  • 2 oz carrot
  • 4 oz frozen corn and peas
  • 4 large egg
  • 0.50 teaspoon salt
  • 0.30 cup water
  • 2 tablespoon oil

Instructions

  1. Wash & cook rice
  2. Cut green onions & carrot
  3. Crack & season eggs
  4. Microwave veggies & fluff rice
  5. Stir fry & season
  6. Plate the fried rice
