Fish Congee Recipe

Description

This is a fish congee recipe from Made With Lau. Congee is a simple and very nutrious dish for all ages. It's gentle enough to serve for the sick, babies, or for when you want an easily digestible meal.

We add fish as our protein in this recipe, but you can add other types as well for more flavor!

Ingredients

  • 10 oz fish
  • 0.50 lb rice
  • 6 cup water
  • 1 oz ginger
  • 2 stalk green onion
  • 0.25 oz cilantro
  • 1 egg (egg whites only)

Instructions

  1. Prepare aromatics
  2. Prepare fish
  3. Wash rice
  4. Cook congee
  5. Add ingredients & seasoning
